xVoice Clone Privacy Policy

xVoice Clone converts authorized voice samples into secure, policy-aware synthetic models. This document details the data lifecycle practices that keep customer recordings and resulting assets private throughout the engagement.

Jump to Policy Back to XM LLC

Effective: March 15, 2024 · Next independent review: September 2024

Data we collect

  • We do not collect, store, or share personal information.
  • We do not create user accounts.
  • We do not track you or use the Identifier for Advertisers (IDFA).
  • We do not use analytics, crash-reporting, or advertising SDKs.
  • We do not request access to Contacts, Photos, Camera, Microphone, Location, Health, or other sensitive data.

Third parties

The App may interact with Apple systems such as the App Store or device services. Apple may process limited device information under its own privacy policies in order to deliver the App and system services. We do not control Apple’s processing of that information.

Children’s privacy

The App is not directed to children under 13 (or the age required in your region). Because we do not collect any data, we do not knowingly collect data from children.

Security

We do not collect or store personal information, so there is no user data on our servers that requires security controls.

Changes to this policy

If xVoice Clone adds features that collect data—such as analytics, crash logs, or login—we will update this policy and the App’s disclosures before those changes go live.
