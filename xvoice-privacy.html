xVoice Clone Privacy Policy

xVoice Clone converts authorized voice samples into secure, policy-aware synthetic models. This document details the data lifecycle practices that keep customer recordings and resulting assets private throughout the engagement.

Jump to Policy Back to XM LLC

Effective: March 15, 2024 · Next independent review: September 2024

Data Collected

  • Customer provides scripted voice samples plus metadata about the authorized speaker and intended usage.
  • Optional redaction dictionary captures terms that must never be reproduced in synthesized audio.
  • No biometric identifiers beyond submitted recordings are collected or derived.

Processing & Storage

  • Samples are encrypted in transit (TLS 1.3) and at rest (AES-256 with HSM-backed key rotation).
  • Training workspaces are isolated per customer and monitored with continuous logging.
  • Raw recordings are purged within 14 days of model delivery, and artifacts are versioned for audit traceability.

Security & Access

  • Access requires multi-factor authentication and is limited to personnel cleared for CJIS/HIPAA data scopes.
  • Every playback, export, or model handoff event is logged centrally and reviewed weekly.
  • Voice fingerprints rendered from synthetic models cannot be reverse engineered to raw audio due to differential privacy noise.

Customer Controls

  • Customers approve script content, retention windows, and downstream distribution rights before work begins.
  • Emergency purge requests honored within 48 hours across all storage tiers.
  • Audit-ready reports delivered upon completion for internal compliance filing.
